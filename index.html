Get to Know Us

Get to Know Us

Pyramid Technical Institute is a recognized vocational training center affiliated with the Council for Technical Education and Vocational Training (CTEVT), Nepal. We are committed to empowering individuals with practical skills and industry-relevant training, helping them secure better career opportunities in Nepal and abroad.

Our Mission: To empower you with cutting-edge training that embodies emotional healing as an applied technology and a wisdom practice. We foster lasting transformation for both practitioners and clients.
